Introduction

This website is dedicated to the X-Man and popular superhero from the Marvel Comics franchise, Wolverine. Also known as Logan or his real name, James Howlett. He was originally an X-Man but then became an Avenger. He also appears in multiple movies and games all produced by either Marvel or its associates such as Disney or Fox.

LORE

Wolverine, real name is James Howlett, was born in the 1800s in Canada. He grew up in a wealthy family, but his life took a dark turn when his father was killed. This triggered a transformation in young James, with his mutation activating, giving him access to a pair of bone claws, and he ran away from home, changing his name to Logan. Logan wandered for years, trying to survive. He fought in wars and struggled with his emerging mutant powers. His life became worse when he was captured by a secret government program. They coated his bones and claws with adamantium, turning him into a dangerous weapon. Much of Logan's past is a blur due to memory loss, so he's often doesnt know of who he really is. He’s lost close people, battled inner demons, and wrestled with his animal-like nature. But, despite his troubled history, he found a purpose withen the x-men, a group of mutants dedicated to peaceful coexistence with humans. Logan’s healing ability makes him nalmost indestructible, but it’s his complex, tormented soul that makes him stand out. Though he’s been moulded by violence, he’s always searching for redemption, trying to do right in a world that often sees him as a danger rather than a person.
